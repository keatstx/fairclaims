Three Engines, One Mission

ThriveAI Ecosystem

Chicago Urban League

AI-augmented knowledge and wellness platform. Provides digital infrastructure from Wellness App to case management systems that power informed decision-making across Chicago's most under-resourced communities.

FairClaims Platform

Intelligent Advocacy Engine

Handles intake, case routing, document management, eligibility screening, and outcome tracking across all 5 pillars. Turns complex medical financial challenges into actionable advocacy workflows.

Guild 2.0

Cooperative Network

Vetted legal, financial, and healthcare professionals at fair, transparent rates. Membership-based cooperative that ensures sustained access to expert guidance without predatory pricing.

The Hub-and-Spoke Model

Proposed to City of Chicago in SB Tech Enablement Grant

Smart Hub in Each Neighborhood

One Smart Hub in each of Chicago's 25 lowest-income neighborhoods, digitally connected and locally staffed.

What Makes Each Hub

  • Locally staffed (CEN team on the ground)
  • Digitally connected (powered by FairClaims Platform)
  • Community-embedded (trusted relationships matter)
  • Supported by Guild 2.0 (expert legal and financial partners)

The Core Principle

"The Hubs don't replace existing organizations. They amplify them."
