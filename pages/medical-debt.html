Pillar 2 of 5

That bill might not be yours to pay.

Medical debt is the #1 cause of bankruptcy in America, responsible for 66.5% of all filings. But much of that debt is inflated, incorrectly billed, or legally unenforceable.

The Debt Machine

A crisis hiding in plain sight.

  • Americans owe at least $220 billion in medical debt. For Black families, the burden is disproportionate: 48% carry medical debt, compared to 34% of white families.
  • In February 2026, Illinois announced it erased $1.1 billion in medical debt for over 500,000 residents, spending just $10 million. Every dollar spent cleared more than $100 in debt.
  • Medical bills are the most common debt in collections nationwide. Even a single unpaid bill can damage your credit, block you from renting an apartment, and follow you for years.
  • Many families don't realize they can dispute medical debt, request itemized bills to catch errors, or negotiate directly with hospitals, especially if they would have qualified for financial assistance.
Your Path to Relief

How FairClaims Helps

  1. Debt Verification — We request itemized bills and verify every charge. Billing errors are common.
  2. Financial Assistance Screening — If you should have qualified for free or discounted care, we go back and apply, even after you've been sent to collections.
  3. Negotiation — We work with hospitals and collection agencies to reduce or eliminate your balance.
  4. Relief Program Connection — We connect you to state, county, and nonprofit debt relief programs you may not know about.
  5. Credit Protection — We help you understand your rights under federal and state law to keep medical debt from destroying your credit.
