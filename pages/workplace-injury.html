You got hurt at work. You deserve every dollar.

Workers' compensation exists to protect you, but the system is designed to pay as little as possible. We're here to make sure you get the maximum.

The Denial Playbook

  • 13-25% of workers' comp claims denied on first attempt
  • Employers sometimes discourage reporting. Insurance may attribute injury to pre-existing conditions.
  • Many workers don't know their rights, don't know they can appeal
  • Without an advocate, injured workers accept lowball settlements

How FairClaims Helps

  1. Immediate guidance
  2. Claim review
  3. Vetted legal connection
  4. Maximization, not just approval
  5. Employer accountability
