If your neighborhood made you sick, someone owes you more than an apology.

From lead paint to contaminated water to PFAS "forever chemicals," communities on Chicago's South and West Side have been disproportionately exposed to industrial toxins for generations.

Environmental Injustice Is Medical Financial Injustice

  • Camp Lejeune: 410,000+ admin claims and nearly 3,000 lawsuits
  • AFFF firefighting foam with PFAS chemicals contaminating water near fire stations, airports
  • Chicago's South/West Side in shadow of industrial corridors, legacy lead contamination
  • Mass tort claims: families dramatically underrepresented due to lack of access

How FairClaims Helps

  2. Claim eligibility screening
  3. Trusted legal connection
  4. Ongoing monitoring
  5. Community-wide advocacy
