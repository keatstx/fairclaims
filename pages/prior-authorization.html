Your doctor said yes. Your insurer said no. We say: fight back.

Prior authorization, the process where insurers require advance approval before covering a treatment, delays care for 94% of patients and causes 82% to abandon treatment entirely.

The Denial Machine

  • 2024: Medicare Advantage insurers processed nearly 53 million prior auth requests, denied 4.1 million.
  • Only 11.5% were appealed. But 80.7% of those were overturned.
  • 61% of physicians say AI tools are increasing denial rates.
  • AMA: 29% of doctors say prior auth led to serious adverse event for a patient.
  • Average physician's office spends 13 hours per week on prior auth paperwork.

  4. We escalate when needed
  5. We teach resilience
